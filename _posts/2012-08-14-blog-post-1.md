The Hydro-Env Modelling Group at CityUHK is proud to announce that two of our PhD students, Miss Dongxue Zheng and Miss Yabi Huang, achieved top honors at the 2026 (10th) Hydro-Environmental Simulation and Prediction Academic Forum.

Representing our group under the supervision of Prof. Xuneng Tong, both students competed in a highly rigorous oral presentation session featuring top scholars from across the country. Their performance was met with distinction:

* **Miss Dongxue Zheng** was awarded the **First-Class Prize** for her outstanding presentation.
* **Miss Yabi Huang** was recognized with the **Second-Class Prize**.

This forum is a major national-level event in our field, drawing over 300 in-person attendees and more than 10,000 online participants. Beyond these student successes, Prof. Xuneng Tong served on the conference executive committee, reinforcing our group’s ongoing commitment to leadership in regional academic collaboration alongside elite institutions like Tsinghua University, Harbin Institute of Technology, and Hohai University.

We extend our warmest congratulations to Miss Zheng and Miss Huang for their hard work and excellence, which continue to showcase the high-impact research being developed within our group.
