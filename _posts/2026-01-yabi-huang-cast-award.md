The School of Energy and Environment (SEE) at City University of Hong Kong is pleased to announce that Miss Yabi Huang, a first-year PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Xuneng Tong, has been selected for the 2025 Youth Science and Technology Talent Development Program, organized by the China Association for Science and Technology (CAST).

This prestigious program, launched for the first time in Hong Kong, aims to nurture outstanding young scientific talents and foster international academic exchange. Miss Huang was among fewer than 50 PhD students across the region to receive this honour for her research on Environmental Fate Simulation, specifically her project titled “Quantifying Human and Veterinary Contributions to Riverine Antibiotic Pollution”. She will receive a conference grant of RMB 40,000 to support her participation in international academic conferences.
