We are a research team based at the [School of Energy and Environment](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/see/) and [State Key Laboratory of Marine Environmental Health](https://www.cityu.edu.hk/sklmeh/), City University of Hong Kong. The Hydro-Env Modelling Group at CityUHK specializes in investigating the complex dynamics of aquatic environments. Our research core lies in the integration of advanced process-based modelling and data-driven computational approaches to understand and predict the transport and fate of microbial and chemical contaminants. By bridging the gap between theoretical modeling and field-scale applications, we strive to provide robust scientific insights for sustainable water quality management and ecosystem resilience.

<h2 class="archive__subtitle">Key Research Interests</h2>

<div style="margin-top: 20px;">
  <p><strong>Hydro-Environmental & Ecological Modelling</strong><br>
  We specialize in developing and applying advanced hydrodynamic, water quality, and ecological models. By integrating process-based mechanistic simulations with data-driven techniques, we significantly enhance the predictive accuracy and robustness of models across complex aquatic systems.</p>

  <p><strong>Transport & Fate of Contaminants</strong><br>
  We investigate the environmental behavior of microbial and chemical contaminants across watershed-to-coastal continua. Our research elucidates the mechanisms of pollutant transport, transformation, and ultimate fate, providing a rigorous scientific foundation for regional ecological risk assessment and mitigation.</p>

  <p><strong>Pioneering Digital Waters</strong><br>
  We are advancing the paradigm of Digital Waters by creating high-fidelity, data-integrated replicas of aquatic systems. By fusing real-time sensing with predictive modelling, we provide a dynamic digital environment for stress-testing scenarios and optimizing management strategies.</p>

  <p><strong>Multi-source Big Data & Data Mining</strong><br>
  Leveraging heterogeneous environmental datasets, we employ advanced data mining and machine learning to extract critical spatiotemporal patterns and identify non-linear relationships between environmental stressors and ecological responses.</p>

  <p><strong>Remote Sensing & Hydro-Env Modelling Synergy</strong><br>
  We bridge macro-scale remote sensing with high-fidelity numerical models. By developing advanced parameter inversion algorithms, we integrate satellite, UAV, and ground-based data into our simulations, enabling comprehensive monitoring and high-precision early warning.</p>
</div>
